### FAQ Items

#### Q1
**Question:** How long does a project take?
**Answer:** Starter pages go live in 3–5 days. Multi-page Business & SEO sites typically take 2–4 weeks. Enterprise projects get a dedicated timeline scoped during our first call.

#### Q2
**Question:** Can you guarantee first-page Google rankings?
**Answer:** No one can guarantee a specific ranking position — anyone who says otherwise is lying. What we guarantee is a technically flawless, search-optimized foundation. Clean code, fast load times, structured data, and keyword-targeted content. We've consistently placed brands at the top of their local niche with this approach.

#### Q3
**Question:** Why no templates?
**Answer:** Templates look like templates. Visitors can tell. We build from a blank canvas every time so your site feels like *your* brand, not a reskinned version of someone else's. Unique geometry, animation curves, and interactions that stick in memory.

#### Q4
**Question:** What's the payment structure?
**Answer:** 50% upfront to start. The remaining 50% after you've reviewed a working test deployment and approved it. Enterprise projects can be structured around milestones. No surprises.

#### Q5
**Question:** What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?
**Answer:** When your customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI for a recommendation in your industry, GEO ensures you're the name that comes up. We structure your content and online presence so AI systems cite and recommend your business.

#### Q6
**Question:** Do you handle hosting and maintenance?
**Answer:** We can. We deploy on Vercel or similar edge platforms for maximum speed and uptime. Post-launch maintenance and updates are available as an add-on or part of Enterprise plans.

## Voice & Copy Rules (For Implementing Agent)

1. **No banned phrases.** Do not add "game-changer", "seamless", "robust", "leverage", "streamline", "unlock your potential", or any phrase from the banned list.
2. **Use contractions.** "We'll" not "We will." "You're" not "You are."
3. **Active voice only.** "We build" not "Websites are built by us."
4. **Specific over vague.** "3–5 days" not "quickly." "₹5,000" not "affordable."
5. **No em dashes for drama.** Use periods or restructure.
6. **One idea per sentence.** Short first, long second.
7. **CTAs name the action.** "Send Message", "Book a Call", "Get Started" — not "Learn More" or "Submit."
